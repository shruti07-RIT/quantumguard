🌾 About QuantumGuard

Built for India's Farmers

QuantumGuard is a blockchain-powered digital identity platform designed to solve the identity and credit-access problem faced by 140+ million Indian farmers. We combine Ethereum smart contracts, IPFS document storage, and government scheme integration into one simple portal.

🔐
Tamper-Proof Identity
Every farmer's identity is hashed with SHA-256 and stored permanently on the Ethereum blockchain — no middlemen, no fraud.
📄
Decentralised Documents
Land records, Aadhaar scans, and bank passbooks are pinned to IPFS via Pinata — permanently accessible with a unique CID.
🏦
Instant Loan Access
Verified farmers get pre-assessed loan eligibility — banks and NBFCs can trust your digital identity without lengthy paperwork.
Our Mission

To give every Indian farmer a verifiable, digital economic identity — enabling fair credit access, government benefit delivery, and land record transparency through open blockchain technology.

⚡ Platform Features

Everything a Farmer Needs

From digital identity to loan approval — one platform built on trust and transparency.

🆔
Blockchain Identity (SHA-256)
Your identity hash is permanently stored on Ethereum. No one can alter, duplicate, or falsify your farmer ID.
🔒
AES-256 Aadhaar Encryption
Raw Aadhaar numbers are never stored. We encrypt your sensitive data before saving it anywhere — local or cloud.
📄
IPFS Document Storage
All your documents — Aadhaar, land records, bank passbook — are pinned to IPFS via Pinata for tamper-proof storage.
🏛️
Auto Scheme Matching
Based on your land, crop, income and location, we auto-match you to PM-KISAN, PMFBY, KCC and 9 other schemes.
💰
Instant Loan Eligibility
Your profile is scored against 6 criteria. Get real-time loan pre-approval for Kisan Credit Card or PM Kisan Yojana.
📱
QR Identity Card
Your verified QR code links to your blockchain identity, documents and profile. Share with any bank or government office.
